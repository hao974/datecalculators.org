Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is this date calculator?

Our date calculator is extremely accurate and accounts for leap years, different month lengths, and other calendar nuances. The calculations are based on the standard Gregorian calendar and use JavaScript's built-in Date object for reliability.

Does the calculator include both the start and end dates in its calculations?

By default, our calculator is inclusive of the end date but not the start date. This means if you calculate from January 1 to January 10, it will return 9 days. This follows the standard convention for calculating elapsed time.

Can I calculate business days instead of calendar days?

Currently, our calculator focuses on calendar days. We're working on adding a business days calculator in a future update that will exclude weekends and optionally holidays.

Is my data saved or shared when I use this calculator?

No, all calculations are performed locally in your browser. We don't store, collect, or share any of the dates you enter. Your privacy is important to us!

Can I use this calculator for historical dates before 1900?

Yes, our calculator can handle dates from 0001-01-01 forward. However, please note that very old dates may be subject to calendar system differences (Julian vs. Gregorian) that can affect exact accuracy for historical date calculations.
